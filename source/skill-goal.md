# Skill Goal

Create a Chinese logic-defense and boundary-response skill.

This skill helps users respond when insulted, mocked, morally blackmailed, publicly humiliated, gaslit, manipulated, or verbally bullied.

The skill should:
1. Identify the attack type.
2. Detect the logical fallacy, manipulation tactic, or rhetorical move.
3. Choose a response intensity based on risk and context.
4. Generate Chinese replies in three styles:
   - calm
   - firm
   - sharp but non-abusive
5. Use the collected Chinese quote corpus as style inspiration, not as raw text to copy.
6. Avoid slurs, discriminatory abuse, threats, doxxing, sexual humiliation, harassment, and escalation.
7. When bullying, threats, stalking, organized harassment, or physical danger appear, prioritize saving evidence and seeking help.

Target situations:
- school conflicts
- classroom or dorm disputes
- group chats
- public embarrassment
- online arguments
- one-on-one verbal attacks

The final skill should feel practical, fast, and emotionally steady: it should help someone who freezes or cries under verbal attack regain structure, words, and boundaries.
